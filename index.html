About Us

Dentpark is a trusted dental clinic located in Hari Nagar Ashram, New Delhi, dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate dental care to patients of all ages. Led by Dr. Vedika and supported by a warm, skilled team, we combine clinical excellence with a patient-first approach that makes every visit comfortable and reassuring. With a stellar rating of 4.9 out of 5 from over 183 satisfied patients, we have built our reputation on transparency, precision, and genuine care.

At Dentpark, we believe that a healthy smile can transform your confidence and quality of life. Whether you need a routine check-up, a complex extraction, or a root canal treatment, our team walks you through every step of the procedure so you always feel informed and at ease. Conveniently situated opposite Jeewan Hospital in Bhagwan Nagar Phase 2, we welcome patients six days a week and are committed to making world-class dental care accessible right in your neighbourhood.

Our Services

What we offer

🏥

Dental Consultation & Check-up

Comprehensive oral examinations to assess your dental health and plan the right course of treatment tailored to your needs.

💊

Tooth Extraction

Safe and precise extraction of damaged, infected, or wisdom teeth including third molar extractions, performed with minimal discomfort.

🩺

Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

Advanced root canal therapy to save infected or severely damaged teeth, carried out with meticulous care for a pain-free recovery.

📋

Dental Cleaning & Scaling

Professional cleaning to remove plaque, tartar, and stains, keeping your gums healthy and your smile fresh.

💡

Restorative Dentistry

High-quality fillings, crowns, and other restorative procedures to repair and strengthen damaged teeth and restore your natural bite.

⚕️

Cosmetic Dental Treatments

Smile enhancement procedures designed to improve the appearance of your teeth and boost your confidence every day.
